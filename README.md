📌 Overview

The US Candy Distributor Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze and monitor the performance of a candy distribution business across the United States. The dashboard provides detailed insights into sales, profit, product performance, factory contribution, and regional analysis using interactive visualizations and filters.

This project helps businesses make data-driven decisions by tracking key performance indicators and identifying important sales trends.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance
Monitor yearly sales trends
Compare division-wise sales contribution
Identify top-performing factories and products
Track target achievement
Perform geographic sales analysis
📊 Dashboard Features
🔹 KPI Cards

The dashboard displays important business metrics:

Total Sales: 141.78K
Total Profit: 93.44K
Total Units Sold: 39K
Profit Margin %: 0.66
Target Achievement: 3.15

These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall business performance.

🔹 Sales Trend Analysis

A line chart is used to analyze sales trends from 2021 to 2024.
This helps in understanding:

Business growth patterns
Seasonal sales changes
Performance fluctuations over time
🔹 Division-wise Sales Distribution

A donut chart displays sales contribution by different divisions:

Chocolate
Sugar
Other

This visualization helps compare the revenue generated by each division.

🔹 Factory Performance Analysis

A horizontal bar chart compares total sales generated by factories such as:

Lot's O'
Wicked Choccy's
Secret Factory
The Other Factory

This helps identify the best-performing factories.

🔹 Geographic Sales Analysis

A map visualization displays sales locations using latitude and longitude data.
This feature helps analyze regional sales distribution across the United States.

🔹 Product-wise Sales Analysis

A bar chart is used to compare sales generated by different candy products.
This helps identify:

Best-selling products
High-demand products
Product performance trends
🔹 Sales vs Target Analysis

A clustered bar chart compares:

Actual Sales
Target Sales

This helps evaluate whether business targets are being achieved across different divisions.

🔹 Detailed Data Table

The dashboard includes a detailed table containing:

Product Name
Division
Factory
Total Units
Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin %

Conditional formatting is used to highlight important performance values.

🎛 Filters & Interactivity

Interactive slicers are included for:

Division
Factory
Region
Year (2021–2024)

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard to perform detailed analysis.
