# 🍬 US Candy Distributor Dashboard – Power BI

## 📌 Overview
The US Candy Distributor Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, product distribution, and regional insights for a candy distribution business. This dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make informed decisions using data visualization and real-time filtering.

---

# 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI                | Value   |
|--------------------|---------|
| Total Sales        | 141.78K |
| Total Profit       | 93.44K  |
| Total Units Sold   | 39K     |
| Profit Margin %    | 0.66    |
| Target Achievement | 3.15    |

---

# 📈 Dashboard Insights

## 🔹 Sales Trend Over Time
- Displays sales growth trends from 2021 to 2024.
- Helps identify seasonal patterns and sales performance over time.
- Useful for forecasting and strategic planning.

## 🔹 Total Sales by Division
- Visual representation of sales contribution by product division.
- Chocolate division contributes the highest share of total sales.
- Helps compare business performance across divisions.

## 🔹 Sales by Factory
- Displays factory-wise sales performance.
- Identifies top-performing manufacturing units.
- Useful for operational and supply chain analysis.

## 🔹 Sales by Product Name
- Highlights top-selling candy products.
- Helps identify high-demand products and customer preferences.
- Supports inventory and product planning decisions.

## 🔹 Sales by Location (Map Visualization)
- Geographic representation of factory sales using latitude and longitude.
- Helps analyze regional sales distribution across the United States.
- Useful for logistics and regional market analysis.

## 🔹 Total Sales vs Target Sales
- Compares actual sales with target sales by division.
- Helps evaluate business performance and goal achievement.
- Useful for performance tracking and management reporting.

## 🔹 Detailed Sales Table
The dashboard includes a detailed data table showing:
- Product Name
- Division
- Factory
- Units Sold
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %

Conditional formatting is applied to highlight performance trends.

---

# 🎛 Interactive Features
The dashboard includes multiple interactive filters:
- Division Filter
- Factory Filter
- Region Filter
- Year Filter (2021–2024)

These filters allow users to dynamically analyze data based on selected conditions.
